Who We Are

The Nest Refugee Home

We are a ministry of Owen Sound Alliance Church dedicated to welcoming refugee claimants with the love of God into our home, lives, and community. We provide transitional housing and connection to friendship, support, and resources within our community.

Through prayer, donations, and active involvement, the church creates a safe and welcoming environment where newcomers are supported and able to access the resources they need. This ministry allows us, the church, to extend practical care and community to those starting a new life in Canada.

The Nest is more than just a house — it is a place of belonging, hope, and new beginnings for people who have journeyed far and need a community to call home.

Our Mission

How We Serve Newcomers

We believe every person deserves dignity, safety, and community. The Nest lives out this belief in four practical ways.

🏡

Transitional Housing

We provide a safe, warm home for refugee claimants while they navigate their new life in Canada and work toward stable, long-term housing.

🤝

Friendship & Connection

Newcomers are welcomed into the fabric of our community — sharing meals, friendships, and life alongside church families and neighbours.

📋

Practical Support

We connect residents with essential resources including legal aid referrals, employment support, language programs, and social services.

🙏

Prayer & Spiritual Care

Prayer is the foundation of everything we do. We uphold our residents and volunteers in prayer, trusting God to guide each journey.
